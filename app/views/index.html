Our UR standards

We are creating a set of quality, ethical and safeguarding standards.

Every user researcher working in DfE must always meet these standards.

  • Participant safeguarding
  • Personal data handling

Also in development are standards for Research planning, Safety during research and Inclusive research.

Your work must also always meet the Government Service Standard.
