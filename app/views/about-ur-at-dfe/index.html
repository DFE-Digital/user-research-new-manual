Our mission patch

If you're a DfE user researcher, civil servant or contractor, you can get a mission patch sticker to proudly stick on your laptop.

Our UR community designed this sticker to represent us: otters are collaborative, adaptable, effective as individuals but also have a strong sense of community.

You can get your sticker from the community lead in your office or contact Tom Adams to send you one in the post
