We want this to be the best place in government to deliver user research, and whether you're a new civil servant in a new permanent role, or a contractor with us for a shorter amount of time, you'll be made to feel very welcome. You line manager and team will ensure you know what you need to about the department and your specific service or project. If there is a lead user researcher in your portfolio, you'll meet them in your first day or two to learn about your service area and users. It's also important that you know about how we think about user research across the Department, and that you join up with our community of practice. Here are some links and guidance to get you started.
