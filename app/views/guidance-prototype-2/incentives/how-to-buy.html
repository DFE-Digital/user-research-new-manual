    In exceptional circumstances (e.g. you need a different type of monetary incentive not provided by our normal supplier) you may use a different supplier. This must be agreed with research operations and approved in writing by the head of user research.

    They will ask you for: - budget approval from your team and a purchase order number. Your delivery manager will help you with this. - approval to use monetary incentives from a lead user researcher or head of user researcher.
