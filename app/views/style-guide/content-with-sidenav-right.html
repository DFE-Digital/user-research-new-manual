Examples of GOV Design System components

Warning text

! Warning Use to warn users about something important, such as legal consequences of an action or lack of action.

Inset text

Use to differentiate a block of text from the content that surrounds it, for example quotes, examples, additional information about the page

Accordian

About this accordian component

Use when evidence it's helpful for the user to see an overview of multiple, related sections of content; choose to show and hide sections that are relevant to them; look across information that might otherwise be on different pages. Accordions can also work well for people who use a service regularly, for example who need to do familiar tasks quickly. Test with users to decide if using an accordion outweighs the potential problems with hiding content.

Help with this details component
Make a page easier to scan by letting users reveal more detailed information only if they need it.

Notification

Important

A notification banner lets you tell the user about something thats not directly relevant to the thing they're trying to do on that page of the service. They should be used sparingly. We might use them, for example, to alert people to a pre-election period, or to a major change to the service standard. Otherwise, Inset text or Warning text should be used. Link to something else.
